About

Divinorum is an independent swimwear brand based in BA. Taking aesthetic cues from the sights of the present and the past, Divinorum combines elements of swimwear and lingerie into pieces defined by clean lines and a subtle color palette.
We don't believe in swimming seasons and hope our customers create some sort of adventure in finding a place to swim, no matter the time of year.
With a strong focus on both technical production and quality, Divinorum collections are entirely designed and made in BA using premium, locally sourced materials.
